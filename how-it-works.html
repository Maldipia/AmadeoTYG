‚ùì Common Questions

How do I track my order?
After placing your order, save your Order ID. The merchant will contact you directly via phone/text to confirm delivery time. For questions, you can contact the merchant directly or message us.
What if the product I want is out of stock?
Products marked "Sold Out" are temporarily unavailable. Check back later as merchants restock regularly. You can also browse similar products from other merchants.
Is there a minimum order amount?
Minimum orders depend on each merchant. Most have no minimum for pickup, but delivery may require a minimum amount. Check with the merchant if unsure.
How much is the delivery fee?
Delivery fees vary by merchant and location within Amadeo. Some offer free delivery for orders over a certain amount. The merchant will confirm the total before delivery.
Can I cancel my order?
Contact the merchant directly as soon as possible if you need to cancel. Orders that haven't been prepared yet can usually be cancelled without issue.
What barangays do you deliver to?
We serve all 13 barangays in Amadeo: Poblacion, Dagatan, Halang, Banaybanay, Buho, Maymangga, Lalaan I, Lalaan II, Pangil, Minantok East, Minantok West, Pulong Bunga, and Talon.
