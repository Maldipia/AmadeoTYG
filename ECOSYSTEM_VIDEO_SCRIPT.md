# Amadeo Marketplace: The Ecosystem Loop (1-Minute Video Script)

**Target Audience:** General (Merchants, Customers, Partners)
**Style:** Upbeat, modern cartoon animation (Vyond/Animaker style)
**Music:** Inspiring, positive, modern acoustic track

---

## Scene 1: The Merchant

**Visual:**
- A friendly Filipina merchant named **Maria** stands in front of her small, local store (e.g., a bakery or craft shop). She looks a bit worried, checking her empty phone.
- **On-screen text:** "Struggling to reach new customers?"

**Voiceover (0-5 seconds):**
> "Meet Maria, a local business owner in Amadeo with amazing products, but limited reach."

---

## Scene 2: Discovering the Marketplace

**Visual:**
- Maria is on her phone. A bright, colorful **Amadeo Marketplace logo** appears on her screen with a "Join for FREE" button.
- She taps the button, and her store instantly appears inside a phone screen, looking professional with product photos.
- **On-screen text:** "Zero Risk. 100% Opportunity."

**Voiceover (6-13 seconds):**
> "Then, she joins Amadeo Marketplace. It’s a free, easy-to-use platform that puts her store online in minutes, connecting her to thousands of neighbors."

---

## Scene 3: The Customer

**Visual:**
- A young Filipino customer named **Juan** is relaxing at home, scrolling on his phone.
- He sees Maria’s products on the Amadeo Marketplace app. His eyes light up.
- He taps "Add to Cart" and a shopping bag icon fills up.
- **On-screen text:** "Shop Local. Discover Amadeo."

**Voiceover (14-21 seconds):**
> "Now, meet Juan. He wants to support local businesses but needs a convenient way to shop. He discovers Maria’s store on the marketplace and finds exactly what he’s looking for."

---

## Scene 4: The Incentive (The "Magic Moment")

**Visual:**
- Juan is at the checkout page. He sees two options:
    1.  **COD** (plain, grayed out)
    2.  **GCash/Maya** (bright green, with a sparkling raffle ticket icon next to it)
- He taps the GCash option. A raffle ticket flies out of the phone and into a big, glowing prize box.
- **On-screen text:** "Pay Online, Win Big!"

**Voiceover (22-32 seconds):**
> "At checkout, Juan sees he can pay with cash, OR pay online with GCash to get a FREE entry into our monthly raffle for amazing prizes. He chooses to pay online, and instantly gets his raffle entry!"

---

## Scene 5: The Merchant Gets Paid

**Visual:**
- Back in her store, Maria gets a notification on her phone: **"New Order!"**
- A graphic shows money flowing from Juan’s phone to Maria’s phone, with a small 5% coin branching off to the Amadeo Marketplace logo.
- Maria smiles, looking happy and busy packing the order.
- **On-screen text:** "You only pay when you make a sale."

**Voiceover (33-41 seconds):**
> "Maria gets the order instantly. The platform takes a small 5% fee from the online payment, and Maria keeps the rest. It’s a win-win! She prepares the order for delivery."

---

## Scene 6: The Delivery & The Loop

**Visual:**
- A friendly local rider picks up the package from Maria and delivers it to a happy Juan.
- The scene zooms out to show an animated map of Amadeo. Lines connect Maria’s store, Juan’s house, and other stores and houses, forming a vibrant, glowing network.
- Icons of money, products, and raffle tickets flow through the network.

**Voiceover (42-52 seconds):**
> "The local economy thrives. Customers get great products and a chance to win, while merchants grow their business and reach more people than ever before. It’s a cycle of local success."

---

## Scene 7: Call to Action

**Visual:**
- The Amadeo Marketplace logo appears in the center.
- Two buttons appear below it:
    1.  **For Customers:** "Start Shopping & Winning"
    2.  **For Merchants:** "Join for FREE Today"
- **On-screen text:** "Amadeo Marketplace: Your Community. Your Marketplace."
- Website URL: **amadeomarketplace.com**

**Voiceover (53-60 seconds):**
> "Ready to be a part of it? Whether you’re a business owner or a shopper, join Amadeo Marketplace today and let’s grow together."

---

**End Screen:** Logo and URL and social media icons for Facebook/Instagram.
