üì¶ Orders & Payment

Ano ang accepted payment methods? +
Payment is directly between you and the provider. Most providers accept:
  • Cash on Delivery (COD) - Most common
  • GCash - Many providers accept
  • Bank Transfer - Some providers
Check with the provider when you contact them!
May delivery fee ba? +
Depende sa provider. Each business sets their own delivery policy:
  • Some offer FREE delivery within Amadeo
  • Some have minimum order for free delivery
  • Some charge small delivery fee for far areas
Always confirm with the provider before ordering!
Puwede ba mag-cancel ng order? +
Cancellation policy depends on the provider and the situation:
  • Before preparation/dispatch: Usually okay to cancel
  • After preparation: May have cancellation fee
  • For services: Depends on provider's policy
Best practice: Contact the provider immediately if you need to cancel.
May refund ba kung may problema? +
Refunds are handled by the provider. Generally:
  • Wrong item delivered: Provider should replace or refund
  • Item damaged: Provider should address
  • Service not rendered: Full refund expected
If provider doesn't cooperate, contact us and we'll help mediate. Repeated issues = provider removed from marketplace.
