# A/B Testing Strategy for Amadeo Marketplace Landing Pages

**Objective:** Optimize conversion rates on merchant and customer landing pages through systematic A/B testing of key elements, with primary focus on CTA (Call-to-Action) effectiveness.

---

## üéØ Testing Goals

### **Merchant Landing Page:**
- **Primary Goal:** Increase merchant signup applications
- **Target Conversion Rate:** 15-20% of visitors click "Apply Now"
- **Secondary Goal:** Reduce bounce rate below 40%

### **Customer Landing Page:**
- **Primary Goal:** Increase click-through to main shopping site
- **Target Conversion Rate:** 25-30% of visitors click "Shop Now"
- **Secondary Goal:** Increase raffle awareness and online payment preference

---

## üìä A/B Test Variations

### **Merchant Landing Page Tests**

#### **Test 1: Primary CTA Copy**
- **Control (A):** "Start Selling for FREE"
- **Variant B:** "Join 50+ Amadeo Merchants Today"
- **Variant C:** "Get Your FREE Store in 5 Minutes"
- **Hypothesis:** Social proof or time-based urgency will outperform generic free offer

#### **Test 2: Hero Headline**
- **Control (A):** "Sell to Your Neighbors. Grow Your Business."
- **Variant B:** "Turn Your Products into Profit - Zero Risk, All Reward"
- **Variant C:** "Amadeo's #1 Platform for Local Sellers"
- **Hypothesis:** Benefit-focused or authority-based headlines will increase engagement

#### **Test 3: Pricing Display**
- **Control (A):** "‚Ç±0/month" with detailed breakdown
- **Variant B:** "100% FREE Forever" badge with simplified text
- **Variant C:** Remove pricing card, integrate into benefits section
- **Hypothesis:** Simplified messaging reduces cognitive load and increases conversions

#### **Test 4: CTA Button Color**
- **Control (A):** Orange (#ff9800)
- **Variant B:** Green (#4caf50)
- **Variant C:** Red (#ff5722)
- **Hypothesis:** Green (success color) will outperform orange

---

### **Customer Landing Page Tests**

#### **Test 1: Primary CTA Copy**
- **Control (A):** "Start Shopping & Winning"
- **Variant B:** "Shop Now & Get Your Raffle Entry"
- **Variant C:** "Save 3% + Win Prizes Today!"
- **Hypothesis:** Specific benefit mention will increase click-through

#### **Test 2: Hero Headline**
- **Control (A):** "Shop Local. Save Money. Win Big."
- **Variant B:** "Your Favorite Amadeo Stores, Now Online"
- **Variant C:** "Win a Tagaytay Staycation This Month!"
- **Hypothesis:** Prize-focused headline creates stronger FOMO

#### **Test 3: Raffle Section Position**
- **Control (A):** Raffle section as 2nd section (after features)
- **Variant B:** Raffle section immediately after hero
- **Variant C:** Raffle integrated into hero section
- **Hypothesis:** Earlier raffle visibility increases engagement

#### **Test 4: Value Proposition Order**
- **Control (A):** Save ‚Üí Win ‚Üí Support
- **Variant B:** Win ‚Üí Save ‚Üí Support
- **Variant C:** Support ‚Üí Save ‚Üí Win
- **Hypothesis:** Leading with "Win" creates stronger emotional hook

## üî¨ Testing Methodology

### **Traffic Split:**
- 50/50 split for two-variant tests
- 33/33/34 split for three-variant tests
- Minimum 100 visitors per variant before analysis

### **Success Metrics:**

**Primary Metrics:**
- Click-through rate (CTR) on primary CTA
- Conversion rate (form submissions/signups)
- Time to conversion

**Secondary Metrics:**
- Bounce rate
- Average time on page
- Scroll depth
- Secondary CTA clicks

### **Statistical Significance:**
- Minimum confidence level: 95%
- Minimum test duration: 7 days
- Minimum sample size: 200 visitors per variant
