‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

How much does it cost to join?
Nothing! Joining is 100% FREE. We only charge a 5% commission when you make a sale. If you don't sell, you don't pay anything.
How do I get paid?
You collect payment directly from customers (COD or your own GCash). You're in full control of your money. The 5% commission is settled weekly via GCash.
Who handles delivery?
You handle your own delivery, just like you do now. We just help you get more orders and manage them better. You keep your customer relationships.
What if I already have Facebook/Instagram?
Great! This is an additional channel - not a replacement. More visibility = more sales. Plus you get inventory management that social media doesn't offer.
How long does approval take?
Usually same day! We just need to verify you're a real Amadeo business. Once approved, you can start adding products immediately.
Do I need to be tech-savvy?
Not at all! If you can use Facebook, you can use our dashboard. It's designed to be super simple. Plus we provide free training and support.
What types of businesses can join?
Any legitimate Amadeo business! Water refilling, LPG, food, grocery, services (plumbing, electrical), farm products, and more. As long as you serve Amadeo customers, you're welcome!
Is my business information safe?
Yes! Your data is stored in Google's secure servers. Only you and authorized platform admins can access your business information.
