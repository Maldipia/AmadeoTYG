Local Businesses Are Losing Sales to Big E-Commerce Platforms

  • Small local businesses struggle to reach customers beyond their immediate neighborhood
  • Customers default to national platforms like Lazada & Shopee instead of shopping locally
  • No trusted, local-first marketplace exists specifically for the Amadeo community
  • Result: Local businesses lose revenue and the community loses economic activity

"87% of consumers prefer to support local businesses but lack convenient platforms to do so."
