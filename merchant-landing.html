Frequently Asked Questions

What are the requirements to join?

You must have a registered business located in Amadeo, Cavite, and a valid business permit. That's it!

How do I get paid?

For COD orders, you are paid directly by the customer. For online payments, we transfer your earnings (minus the 5% fee) to your designated bank account or e-wallet on a weekly basis.

Who handles delivery?

You do! You have full control over your delivery process. Customers book and pay their chosen rider directly, so you don't have to worry about platform delivery fees.

What kind of marketing support do you provide?

We provide our new merchants with a starter kit of marketing materials (graphics and post templates) that you can use on your own social media pages for 7 to 30 days. We also regularly feature our merchants on our official Amadeo Marketplace Facebook page.
