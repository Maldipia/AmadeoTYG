Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your information

Last Updated: December 1, 2025

1. Introduction

Amadeo Marketplace ("we," "our," or "the Platform") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our platform.

Quick Summary: We only collect information necessary to provide our marketplace services. We never sell your personal data to third parties.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

Data Type Purpose
Name Account identification, order processing
Email Address Account login, order notifications
Phone Number Order updates, delivery coordination
Address Delivery fulfillment
GCash Number (Merchants) Commission settlement

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Device information (browser type, operating system)
  • Usage data (pages visited, time spent)
  • IP address (for security purposes)

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Process and fulfill orders
  • Send order confirmations and updates
  • Facilitate communication between customers and merchants
  • Process merchant commission settlements
  • Improve our platform and services
  • Prevent fraud and ensure security
  • Comply with legal obligations

4. Information Sharing

4.1 With Merchants

When you place an order, we share your name, phone number, and delivery address with the merchant to fulfill your order.

4.2 Service Providers

We may share information with:

  • Google (hosting and data storage via Google Sheets)
  • Email service providers (for notifications)

4.3 We Do NOT

  • Sell your personal information to third parties
  • Share your data for advertising purposes
  • Use your information for purposes unrelated to our marketplace services

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures including:

  • Password encryption (SHA-256 hashing)
  • Secure HTTPS connections
  • Access controls to limit data access
  • Regular security reviews

Note: While we strive to protect your information, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

6. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as:

  • Your account is active
  • Necessary to provide our services
  • Required by law (e.g., transaction records for tax purposes)

You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access - Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction - Update or correct inaccurate information
  • Deletion - Request deletion of your account and data
  • Opt-out - Unsubscribe from marketing communications

8. Cookies

We use minimal cookies for:

  • Session management (keeping you logged in)
  • Remembering your preferences

We do not use tracking cookies for advertising purposes.

9. Children's Privacy

Our platform is not intended for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes via email or platform notification.
